11th Feb 2021

0930 - 1100

The CNY celebration will be incorporated into the school programme on Thursday, 11 February 2021. Students are to report to school as usual at 7.45 am for flag-raising and are encouraged to wear their ethnic clothes.

Lessons will be conducted as per normal from 8am to 9.30am, followed by a virtual CNY concert in mentor classrooms to celebrate the occasion. Students are advised to have their breakfast before lessons start as there will be no recess break. They will be dismissed in batches if they do not have any school-based activities.
