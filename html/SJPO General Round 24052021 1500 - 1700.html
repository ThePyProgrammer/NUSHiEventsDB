The Singapore Junior Physics Olympiad (SJPO) 2021 is a Physics competition organized by the Institute of Physics, Singapore (IPS) for secondary school students. This annual competition aims to encourage the study and appreciation of Physics beyond the school curriculum, by providing an opportunity for students to solve novel questions that require critical and inventive thinking. Medals (Gold, Silver, Bronze and Honourable Mention) will be presented to deserving students in this competition and top students will have a chance to participate in the Singapore Physics Olympiad (SPhO) 2021.
